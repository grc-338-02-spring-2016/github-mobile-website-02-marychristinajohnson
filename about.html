About Me

I am a third year student at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Communication with a concentration in Design Reproduction Technology.

I love traveling and being creative, and I have a passion for all things beautiful —especially beautiful type, beautiful clothes and beautiful people. I enjoy collaboration and I hope to utilize my background in leadership development within the Graphic Arts industry.
